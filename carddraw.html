Description
Draw cards from your deck to create equations and earn points for your final score.
Use ability cards to discover strategies and synergies to earn even more points.
Spend points in the shop to expand your deck with new cards and abilities.
Your score is your currency: points you spend in the shop are deducted from your score, you should carefully determine when it's time to stop buying cards and start accumulating points.
