Description
SQUAREISH is a minimalist puzzle platformer where you control a cute little square that can squish down to get through narrow passages or squish back up to jump.
Solve puzzles to collect pixels in 35+ colorful levels with gravity portals, lever switches, pushable boxes, teleports, spikes, pressure plates, sliding doors, and hidden secrets!
Features
35+ colorful puzzle platformer levels to unlock and explore
Minimalist single-room hand-crafted puzzles
Gravity portals, lever switches, pushable boxes, teleports, spikes, pressure plates, sliding doors
Secrets hidden in multiple levels
Progressively more challenging single-player puzzles
No time limits, no enemies, no lives - just you and the puzzles
Cute little square character with a unique jump mechanic
Original soundtrack
